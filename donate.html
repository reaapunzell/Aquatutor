About Us

Princess Care is committed to promoting menstrual health and hygiene for girls and women across the globe. We partner with NGOs, organizations, communities, and companies to ensure that no girl or woman is left without access to essential sanitary products.

Our mission is to break the silence around menstruation and empower girls and women with the resources and education they need to manage their periods with dignity and comfort.
