Menstrual Health Information

Understanding Your Menstrual Cycle

The menstrual cycle is a natural process that occurs in the female body, typically lasting about 28 days. It involves hormonal changes that prepare the body for potential pregnancy.

Common Symptoms

  • Cramps
  • Bloating
  • Mood changes
  • Fatigue

Menstrual Hygiene Tips

  1. Change your sanitary product regularly
  2. Wash hands before and after changing products
  3. Choose the right product for your flow
  4. Stay hydrated and maintain a balanced diet
