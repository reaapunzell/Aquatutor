Our Story

Aquatutor bridges the gap between technology and public health. By empowering communities, enhancing sanitation, and promoting sustainable water practices, it paves the way for a healthier, more connected world. We believe that Aquatutor has the potential to revolutionize the way we approach sanitation infrastructure management and community engagement, and we look forward to developing this innovative solution at the hackathon.
